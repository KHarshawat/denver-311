This is a dynamic infographic that visualizes important information about the 311 service in the City and County of Denver. Each chart below attempts to help answer important questions using data about cases in the 311 system. The information presented here is a representation of Open Data provided by the City and County of Denver.

Last updated March 2014

Topic distribution
